Rewarewa Honey | 500g

This distinctive sub-alpine flower is often called the New Zealand Honeysuckle. The long succulent flowers only blossom for a short period to produce a flavour that is distinctly of New Zealand. Rewarewa is a great natural sweetener for hot drinks and also delicious when eaten by itself or used in baking.
